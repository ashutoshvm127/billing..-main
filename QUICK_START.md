# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - BillSync

Get started with your new billing software in 2 minutes!

## Step 1: Login

When you open the app, you'll see the login page.

**Demo credentials** (enter anything):
```
Full Name: Your Name
Company Name: Your Company
Email: your@email.com
Password: anything
```

Click **"Sign In"** to proceed.

## Step 2: Explore the Dashboard

You'll see your main dashboard with:
- **Total Revenue** card
- **Total Invoices** card
- **Overdue Amount** card
- Quick action buttons

## Step 3: Create Your First Invoice

1. Click **"New Invoice"** button
2. Fill in client details:
   - Client name (e.g., "Acme Corp")
   - Client email
   - Due date
3. Add items:
   - Description (e.g., "Web Design Services")
   - Quantity (e.g., 1)
   - Rate (e.g., 5000)
4. Click **"Create Invoice"**
5. You'll see it in the invoices table!

## Step 4: View Invoice Preview

1. Click the **eye icon** next to an invoice
2. See the professional invoice template
3. Click **printer icon** to print
4. Click **X** to close

## Step 5: Add Clients

1. Go to **Clients** page
2. Click **"Add Client"**
3. Fill in:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Phone (optional)
   - Company (optional)
   - Address (optional)
4. Click **"Add Client"**

## Step 6: Record Payments

1. Go to **Payments** page
2. Click **"Record Payment"**
3. Fill in:
   - Invoice number (e.g., "INV-2024-0001")
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Date
   - Status
4. Click **"Record Payment"**

## Step 7: View Reports

1. Go to **Reports** page
2. See real-time analytics:
   - Revenue metrics
   - Monthly revenue chart
   - Invoice status breakdown
   - Recent invoices

## Step 8: Customize Settings

1. Click on your **profile avatar** (top right)
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Adjust:
   - Notification preferences
   - Security settings
   - Data management

## ❌ Clear Data

If you want to start fresh:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **"Clear All Data"**
4. Confirm deletion

⚠️ **Warning**: This cannot be undone!

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Lost data after refresh?**
- Check browser console (F12)
- Clear browser cache
- localStorage should persist data
- Try in incognito mode

**Invoice not saving?**
- Fill all required fields (client, email, due date, items)
- Item descriptions must not be empty
- Check browser localStorage is enabled

**Can't login?**
- Try a different email format
- Password can be anything in demo mode
- Check if cookies are enabled

**Dark mode not working?**
- Refresh the page
- Check browser theme settings
- Try another browser
